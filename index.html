Frequently asked questions

What should I do when I find a bug ?

To help us improve iTweakHub, please DM us or email-us with a screenshot/screen recording when you find a bug.

Is iTweakHub really free ?

YES ! iTweakHub is totally free and without ads ! Our team does not earn any money with iTweakHub.

Can I contribute for iTweakHub ?

Absolutely! You can contribute to our GitHub repository. Your username or @ will be displayed on the credits page.

Which OS is supported ?

iTweakHub is compatible with iOS and iPadOS only. For an optimal experience, iOS 13 or later is recommended for the web version and iOS 16 or later is required for the native version.

How can I donate for iTweakHub ?

Actually, we haven't a way to receive your donates but it coming soon !
