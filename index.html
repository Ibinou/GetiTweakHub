Frequently asked questions

Is iTweakHub really free ?

YES ! iTweakHub is totally free and withou ads ! Our team does not earn any money with iTweakHub.

Can I contribute for iTweakHub ?

Absolutely! You can contribute to our GitHub repository. Your username or @ will be displayed on the credits page.

Which OS is supported ?

iTweakHub is compatible with iOS, iPadOS, and M1+ Macs only. For an optimal experience, iOS 13 or later is recommended. M1+ Macs can run iOS apps using their IPA files.
